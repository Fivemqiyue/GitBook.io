# Publish your docs

## Share your docs online

Once you've created your documentation, you can publish it to make it accessible to your team or the public.

### Publishing options

1. **Public** - Anyone can access your documentation
2. **Private** - Only invited members can access
3. **Unlisted** - Anyone with the link can access

### How to publish

1. Go to your Space settings
2. Navigate to **Publish** section
3. Choose your visibility option
4. Click **Publish**

Your documentation will be available at:
```
https://your-org.gitbook.io/your-space
```
